Biography

I'm Gissel Salazar and I'm a Software Developer

I live Denver, CO. I have a background in Property and Casualty Insurance. I was in the industry for nearly 10 years before deciding to take a break and focus on spending more time with my family. Since then, I have had a desire to learn something new. I have always enjoyed fixing things and decided coding always needs “fixing”. With that said, I enrolled in the Emeritus MIT xPRO Full Stack Development course and have loved it! Although it comes with many challenges, the feeling of satisfaction when things finally get figured out is indescribable! When I am not coding, I enjoy spending time with Jesus! I love being able to worship on Sunday mornings with my husband and two kids. We also enjoy traveling and look forwards to seeing other countries, but for now Cancun, Mexico is my favorite. I look forward to pursuing a career in coding. I like the challenge and always strive to persevere!
